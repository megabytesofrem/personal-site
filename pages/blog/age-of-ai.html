The Age of AI

#opinion_piece, #ai

Today I woke up, checked my YouTube feed and saw a video by the Primeagen that really stood out to me.

We live in an age where we can just prompt a large-language model1 to "create a webapp to store, for e.g a list of first and last names" and have the AI model "autocomplete" the whole project. While at first this may sound appealing: there are a few major downsides.

  1. Privacy - Every query is being stored and can be used for training. This can be a disaster if the data is sensitive in nature.
  2. Accuracy - AI tends to hallucinate a lot and can be very misleading
  3. Learning - Overusing AI results in a dependence on the AI and inhibits learning
  4. Environmental - AI consumes as much power as a small country2


Instead of prompting an AI, take time to research the topics yourself. You may learn something new.

We survived 10+ years without AI.
