Welcome!

Welcome to my site! This is intended to be a space where I post updates on my projects and share my thoughts in my blog. I hope you enjoy your stay!


Q: Who are you?

I go by Charlotte (she/her/they). I'm a 24 year old software developer from the UK. My projects include a small compiler and VM, an IRC client for the Nintendo 3DS and this site. My music taste is varied and ranges from Cavetown to femtanyl.

I use Fedora Linux as my main operating system and have a distaste for proprietary software (especially Windows). You can click here to see my setup.

Q: Whats up with the design?

This website is designed to be both simple and pleasing to view and nostalgic. The banner image is from Serial Experiments: Lain taken from this site, and converted to black and white with imagemagick*. All other assets were created by myself.

The command used*: convert input.jpg -colorspace Gray output.jpg.
